
Working with HDF5 Attributes
****************************

HDF5 attributes are a convenient mechanism for attaching metadata to
HDF5 objects (datasets, groups, datatype objects). |product| has two functions
for reading and writing HDF5 attributes, `h5readAttribute` and
`h5writeAttribute`. The latter does double duty for creating and updating
such attributes.
